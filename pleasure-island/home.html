Welcome to Pleasure Island, North Carolina!

Discover our beaches, our history, & our hospitality

Plan your summer vacation at Pleasure Island, home to some of the most beautiful North Carolina beaches.

Enjoy the sand, surf, and our quaint, historic beach towns of Carolina Beach, Kure Beach, and historic Fort Fisher.

Come for the day, a weekend getaway, or a long beach vacation. You're sure to discover why the summer never ends on Pleasure Island!
